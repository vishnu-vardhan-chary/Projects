About Employee Management System

The Employee Management System (EMS) is a web application designed to streamline the process of managing employee data. It allows users to add, view, edit, and delete employee information, as well as track important details such as contact information, department, and job title. This system helps organizations improve efficiency and organization in their HR processes.

Here are some key features of EMS:

  • Manage employee information: Add, view, edit, and delete employee profiles.
  • Track employee details: Contact information, department, job title, etc.
  • Enhanced organization: Keep all employee data centralized and easily accessible.
  • Search employees: Search employees based on name, address, and salary
